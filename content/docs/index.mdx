## Welcome to Endvoyant

Endvoyant is a Discord companion for **Arknights: Endfield** focused on the repetitive work you
actually want off your plate: daily attendance, account views, profile snapshots, wiki lookup, and
redeem support.

## Start Here

1. Invite the hosted bot or self-host your own instance.
2. Link an Endfield account with [`/add account`](/docs/add).
3. Use [`/settings`](/docs/settings) to set privacy, language, and automation preferences.

After that, the most important day-to-day commands are [`/attendance`](/docs/attendance),
[`/profile`](/docs/profile), [`/development`](/docs/development),
[`/exploration`](/docs/exploration), [`/characters`](/docs/characters), and
[`/events`](/docs/events).
