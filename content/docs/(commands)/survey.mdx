## Survey command

Open Endvoyant’s in-app feedback and product survey hub.

## Usage

```text
/survey
```

## What It Does

- Opens a survey hub inside Discord for product feedback.
- Includes survey sections for multiple-account usage, general suggestions and feedback, and image-generation interest.
- Uses follow-up modal forms for the currently implemented feedback paths.

## Additional Notes

- Survey submissions are sent through the bot’s configured survey webhook.
- In the current source, the active modal submission paths are the suggestions/feedback survey and the image-generation survey.
- This command is meant for product feedback, not account management or live support.
